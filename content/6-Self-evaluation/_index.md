### Internship Self-Evaluation

Throughout my internship at Amazon Web Services Vietnam Co., Ltd. from 17/04/2026 to 10/07/2026, I had the opportunity to learn, develop, and practice applying acquired knowledge to the Capstone Project and future enterprise environments. During the internship, I was exposed to many AWS services such as IAM, EC2, VPC, S3, RDS, CloudFront, Lambda, ECS, CloudWatch, and participated in labs, workshops, and the Capstone Project.

Through the program, I absorbed and improved my knowledge of cloud computing, enhanced system deployment skills in general and on AWS in particular. Reflexes developed over the long internship period such as teamwork, self-study, troubleshooting, and reporting work progress also improved positively. Additionally, participating in workshops and program events helped me expand practical knowledge and gain clearer direction in the Cloud Computing field.

Regarding my work ethic, I always strive to complete assigned tasks on time, proactively learn, comply with program regulations, and actively exchange with mentors and team members to improve learning and work effectiveness.
