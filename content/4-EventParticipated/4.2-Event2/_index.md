### Event Purpose:
Give students new perspectives on the AI wave shaping the future of enterprises, brought to FCAJ Community Day.

### Speaker List:
- **Truong Tran** - AI Solution Sales (Noventiq)
- **Steve Tran** - CTO/Founder (CloudThinker)
- **Trung Vu** - CEO (Revve AI)
- **Anh Dang** - Solution Sales (Noventiq)
- **Nghi Danh** - AI Engineer (Renova Cloud)
- **Kiet Tran** - AI Engineer (AWS Student Builder Group)
- **Bao Phan** - Cloud Engineer (Cloud Kinetics)
- **Nguyen Nguyen** - Cloud Engineer (Cloud Kinetics)
- **Toan Nguyen** - AWS Security Builder

### Key Highlights:
* **Deep Response Engine: From Detection to Automated Incident Response:** An engaging session on how AI-powered systems can go beyond monitoring and alerting to automatically detect, analyze, and handle incidents.
  - *Speaker:* Nhat Tran.
* **Voice Agents: Building Human-like Conversational AI Assistants at Scale:** A deep dive into the future of conversational AI, showing how Voice Agents are becoming more natural, scalable, and capable of delivering real business value.
  - *Speakers:* Trung Vu, Kiet Tran, and Nghi Danh Hoang Hieu.
* **AWS DevOps Agent: Your Always-On Operations Teammate:** A practical session on how AI can support DevOps teams by automating repetitive operational tasks, thereby improving work efficiency.
  - *Speakers:* Bao Phan Kim and Minh Nguyen.
* **AI-Powered Productivity: Workforce Planning for Enterprises with AI:** An interesting perspective on how AI helps enterprises with workforce planning, resource allocation, and more effective decision making.
  - *Speakers:* Truong Tran and Minh Anh Dang Cao.
* **Building Private and Secure MCP Connections with Amazon Q:** An in-depth session on building secure and private connections, emphasizing the importance of Security-First Architecture in modern cloud environments.
  - *Speakers:* Duc Toan Nguyen and Nghi Danh Hoang Hieu.
