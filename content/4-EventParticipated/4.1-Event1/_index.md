### Event Purpose:
Connect with Cloud experts, explore new Cloud & Generative AI applications, experience live demos, and expand networking within the technology ecosystem.

### Speaker List:
- **Vy Lam** - Senior Business Systems Analyst (VPBank)
- **Thao Nguyen** - GenAI Engineer (VIB)
- **Mai Nguyen** - GenAI Engineer (VIB)
- **Uyen Le** - GenAI Engineer (VIB)
- **Anh Pham** - Cloud Consultant (G-AsiaPacific Vietnam)
- **Thinh Nguyen** - Devops Engineer (FCAJ)
- **Tinh Truong** - Platform Engineer (GoTymeX)
- **Duc Dao** - Solutions Architect (Cloud Kinetics)

### Topics Presented:
- **Context Is Everything: Making AI Actually Work for You** – *Tinh Truong*
- **Friendly AI Assistant with Amazon Q** – *Anh Pham*
- **From Edge To Origin: CloudFront as Your Foundation** – *Thinh Nguyen*
- **36 hrs with LotusHacks – Building UTMorpho from Idea to Reality** – *Uyen Le, Pham Ngoc Nguyen, Ngoc Quynh Mai, and Thao Nguyen Phuong*
- **Non-Determinism of "Deterministic" LLM Settings** – *Duc Dao Minh*
- **Enterprise-Grade Multi-Agent System: The Case of Startup Credit Scoring** – *Lam Hoang Cat Vy*

### Key Highlights:
* **Context Is Everything: Making AI Actually Work for You – Tinh Truong:** Emphasized the role of context in helping AI generate accurate, relevant responses.
* **Friendly AI Assistant with Amazon Q – Anh Pham:** Introduced how Amazon Q helps developers and businesses increase productivity through an AI assistant integrated with the AWS ecosystem.
* **From Edge To Origin: CloudFront as Your Foundation – Thinh Nguyen:** Shared how Amazon CloudFront optimizes performance and reduces latency for web applications through a global network.
* **36 hrs with LotusHacks – Building UTMorpho from Idea to Reality – Uyen Le, Pham Ngoc Nguyen, Ngoc Quynh Mai, and Thao Nguyen Phuong:** Shared the journey of turning an idea into a complete product in just 36 hours, along with lessons on teamwork and problem solving.
* **Non-Determinism of "Deterministic" LLM Settings – Duc Dao Minh:** Explained why LLMs can still produce different results even when using the same prompt and configuration.
* **Enterprise-Grade Multi-Agent System: The Case of Startup Credit Scoring – Lam Hoang Cat Vy:** Introduced a Multi-Agent AI model coordinating multiple agents to solve startup credit scoring efficiently and at scale.
